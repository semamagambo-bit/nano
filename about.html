Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence in Education

Founded with a vision to provide quality education to the children of Kissa and surrounding communities, Kissa Infants and Primary School has been a beacon of educational excellence for over 15 years.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every child deserves access to quality education that nurtures their intellectual, emotional, and physical development. Today, we stand proud as one of the most trusted educational institutions in the region.

  • Established in 2009
  • Trusted by 500+ families
  • Dedicated and experienced staff
What We Stand For

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in early childhood and primary education, nurturing confident, creative, and compassionate individuals who are prepared to make positive contributions to society.

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic education that develops the full potential of each child through innovative teaching methods, a supportive learning environment, and strong partnerships with families and communities.

Our Foundation

Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at Kissa Infants and Primary School.

Love & Care

We create a nurturing environment where every child feels loved, valued, and safe to explore and learn.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in education, behavior, and personal development in everything we do.

Integrity

We promote honesty, respect, and strong moral principles in all our interactions and decisions.

Innovation

We embrace creative thinking and new approaches to learning that prepare children for the future.
