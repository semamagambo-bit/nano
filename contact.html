Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our school.

What are the school hours?

Our school operates from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Classes begin at 8:30 AM and end at 3:30 PM.

Do you offer transportation?

Yes, we offer school bus services for students within Kissa and surrounding areas. Please contact our office for routes and fees.

What is the teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain a low teacher-to-student ratio to ensure personalized attention. For infants and nursery, it's 1:15, and for primary, it's 1:25.

Can I schedule a school visit?

Absolutely! We welcome prospective parents to visit our campus. Please call ahead to schedule an appointment with our admissions team.
