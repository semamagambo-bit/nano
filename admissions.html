How to Apply

Admission Process

We've made the admission process simple and straightforward for parents and students.

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form or visit our school office to pick up a physical form.

2

Document Review

Our admissions team will review your submitted documents and application within 3-5 business days.

3

Assessment

Schedule a date for your child's assessment visit to evaluate their readiness for the program.

4

Enrollment

Upon successful assessment, complete the enrollment process and secure your child's place.

What You Need

Admission Requirements

Please ensure you have the following documents ready for the admission process.

For New Students

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original & copy)
  • Recent passport photos (4 copies)
  • Medical certificate
  • Transfer letter (if from another school)

Parent/Guardian

  • National ID or passport
  • Proof of residence
  • Contact phone numbers
  • Email address
  • Emergency contact details
